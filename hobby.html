Why I prefer to be alone. . .

It wasn't the same since the beginning. It just the circumstances, our mistakes, our failures, and our success and society's view towards us preferentially shapes our personality. I think every single thing that happens in our day to day like how we handle situations, how we overcome society's comments and how we perceive things are majorly responsible for what we choose to accept as reality or simply ignore it . Things are quite similar but little twisty and salty in my case.

It's not good or I think not possible to explain here why I prefer to be alone. But being alone has it's own perks. It allows us to think much deeply on every probalistic possibilities and different relative perspctives leaving us with infinite number of conclusions that sometimes makes no sense. But it worth at the end. I taught myself in this way. Maybe i'm not perfect , doesn't have lot of friends but atleast I know the reality of this external world, I know how to handle situations wisely and above all I know what I am and always be satisfied with it.
